About me

Hello! I'm Dharsha Samarasinha, the artist behind The Canvas by D and my practice is centered on mainly watercolour and acrylic painting. I work across a range of media, including watercolour, pen and ink, pastels,and oils,allowing me to explore both versatility and depth in my work. Studying under several renowned Sri Lankan artists has guided my artistic journey, helping me build strong technical skillsand develop a personal, distinctive style that reflects my vision.
